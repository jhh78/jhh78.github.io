Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q1: How to install the app?

A1: Search for the app on Google Play Store or App Store and press the install button.

Q2: How to withdraw membership?

A2: To withdraw membership, follow these steps:

  • Deleting the app will withdraw your membership.
  • You can withdraw through the withdrawal function provided in the app.

Q3: How to delete the app?

A3: To delete the app, follow these steps:

Android:
  • Select 'Apps' or 'Application Management' from the settings menu.
  • Select the app you want to delete.
  • Press the 'Delete' button.
iPhone:
  • Long press the app you want to delete on the home screen.
  • When the app icon shakes, press the 'X' or '-' button on the app icon.
  • Select 'Delete' to delete the app.
